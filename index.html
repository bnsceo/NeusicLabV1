FAQ

Is NeusicWave free?

Yes. The demo is entirely free and includes everything. Save your projects by upgrading to Pro.

Do I need to be online?

No. All engines run locally in your browser. Internet is only needed to load the page and sync (if you choose).

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes. All apps are mobile-optimized. On smaller screens, panels collapse and stack.

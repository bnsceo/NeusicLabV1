The Neusic signature

From raw sound to finished record.

The tools are not disconnected destinations. Each stage hands the music directly to the next so an idea never loses momentum.

01REC

Capture

Record a microphone or import audio into the session.

02CHP

Chop

Detect tempo and transients, then create slices.

03PAD

Perform

Assign slices to pads and finger-drum patterns.

04MIDI

Compose

Create piano-roll clips, bass lines and melodies.

05ARR

Arrange

Turn loops into sections and shape the full song.

06MIX

Finish

Balance, process, master-check and export.

A serious studio underneath

Built like an instrument, not a dashboard.

Neusic combines the immediacy of a hardware sampler with the structure of a multitrack DAW.

MPC

Sampler & beat lab

Turn any recording into a playable instrument.

  • Transient slicing
  • 16-pad performance
  • Pattern banks A–H
  • Track-routed drums
ARR

Professional arranger

Build complete forms instead of stacking loops.

  • Named sections
  • Loop regions
  • Ripple time editing
  • Multi-clip movement
MIDI

Instrument workstation

Write and arrange playable MIDI phrases.

  • 88-key piano roll
  • Velocity editing
  • Timeline playback
  • Offline rendering
REC

Recording studio

Capture ideas and vocals directly into the song.

  • Microphone recording
  • Waveform clips
  • Monitoring
  • Latency controls
MIX

Mixer & effects

Route each source through its own channel path.

  • Gain and pan
  • Insert effects
  • Stereo metering
  • Master analysis
SAFE

Project system

Keep every session portable and recoverable.

  • .neusic projects
  • IndexedDB autosave
  • Crash recovery
  • Undo and redo
